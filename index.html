Hi! I'm Natasha Mathur

I'm the Director of Data at Maisha Meds, a healthcare tech organization helping improve access to medicine in several sub-Saharan African countries.

Before transitioning into the health policy realm, I worked in American politics. I began my journey as a Data Scientist on Elizabeth Warren's presidential campaign, which I joined in July 2019.

Later, I joined the Biden campaign as a Senior Analytics Engineer, where I focused on early vote data. I continued this role for the Georgia runoff Senate races, leading early vote data engineering efforts.
