Thoughts on Communication Best Practices


Key principles:

1. Nothing should exist only in someone's head

2. Things only get done if it they have a deadline and are assigned to someone.

3. Meetings without an agenda - either internally or client facing - should probably be illegal.

The best system is the one that you and your team will use.

This could be Notion status boards, a running Google doc, Slack, carrier pigeons . . . you do you.
